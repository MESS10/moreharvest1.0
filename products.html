Speciality Fertilizers

Specialty fertilizers include the following: soluble fertilizers (calcium nitrate, magnesium nitrate, magnesium sulphate, potassium nitrate) and foliar sprays.Specialty fertilizers are granular fertilizer particles that restrict the amount of moisture contact and help gradually release fertilizer nutrients, usually over a few weeks or a few months. Specialty fertilizers contribute to advanced fertilizer management programs and to innovative farming systems such as no-tillage farming with single co-situs fertilizer application.

Straight Fertilizers

Moreharvest also trades in straight fertilizers such as ammonium nitrate, urea, single superphosphate (SSP), mono ammonium nitrate (MAP), sulphate of potash (SOP), muriate of potash (MOP) and others. supply only one primary plant nutrient, either N or P or K. On this basis, the fertilizers can be straight nitrogenous, straight Phosphatic or straight Potash fertilizers. For example, Urea provides only N while Potassium Chloride provides only K

Herbicides

Herbicides are chemicals that kill unwanted plants or prevent them from growing.These include: Glyphosate, Atrazine, MCPA, Nicosulfron, Halosulfron,etc

Fungicides

Fungicides are pesticides that kill or prevent the growth of fungi and their spores. They can be used to control fungi that damage plants, including rusts, mildews and blights. They might also be used to control mold and mildew in other settings.These include: Dithane M-45, Copper Oxychloride, Shavit, Bravo, Terbuconazole, Metalaxyl, Ridomil gold,etc.

Insecticides

Insecticides are substances used to kill insects. They include ovicides and larvicides used against insect eggs and larvae, respectively. These include: Dimethoate, Abamectin, Acetamiprid, Acetate, Deltamedthrin, Lambda cyhalothrin, Chlopyriphos, etc.
