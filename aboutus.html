About Us

MOREYEAR INVESTMENT COMPANY (PRIVATE) LIMITED T/A MOREHARVEST FERTILIZERS

Moreyear Investment Company Limited was established and registered in Zimbabwe in October 2018. The company is in the business of fertilizer and agro-chemicals manufacturing, distribution and trading on both local and international markets. In pursuance of this line of business the company has invested in warehouses and factory facilities sitting on about sixteen hactares of land in the Norton Industrial Area, and it is on this property that the company carries out its business of fertilizer and agro-chemicals manufacturing, warehousing and distribution. Moreyear Investment Head Office is located in Harare.


On the fertilizer and agro-chemicals business the company trades as Moreharvest Fertilizers. The company produces both granular and blended high quality, cost effective and agronomic efficient NPK compound fertilizers targeted at both local and regional markets. Apart from manufacturing the company also imports and trades in straight fertilizers such as ammonium nitrate, ammonium sulphate, mono ammonium phosphate, muriate of potash, sulphate of potash and urea. As part of its commitment to ensuring fertilizer use efficiency the company is in the process of establishing in house soil and plant tissue testing facilities which are geared to meet the farmers’ soil testing service requirements timeously. Customer convenience remains central to the company’s business operations and with that in mind the company is currently in the process of establishing product sales points right in the farming areas where farmers can easily collect their agro-inputs using local transport means.
